The Pusher - Moglee (Unofficial Music Video)

The unofficial music video for the song "The Pusher" by the band Moglee.

The concept for this music video was to create a story out of the lyrics. Themes of drug use and criminal activity reoccur throughout. The choice to use dice as the drug substance was made to emphasize the idea of gambling on the wrong side of the law. The highlight of this piece was the use of a variety of locations.
