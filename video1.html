Something to Remember You By

A short film about a undercover agent in a crime ring who's tasked with taking out their leader.

I wrote, produced and directed this piece. I also operated the camera and dressed the set. The goal of this production was to create a compelling story without the use of much action. Primarily I wanted to have powerful lines and dramatic pauses. The lighting setup was all natural and created exactly the atmosphere I was shooting for.
