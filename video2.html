A Long Night

A detective follows his target too closly and unveils a disturbing truth.

This piece was written by Paul Squires. I had various roles as a part of the production crew. The goal of this short film was to create a film noir style dramatic scene. Extremely contrastual lighting was the key to producing this effect. We mostly shot at night but when we did film during the day we blocked all windows with heavy blankets and/or furniture.
