About Me

My name is Zachary Poirier, I am the founder of Digital Wizard Workshop. I have a passion for all things digital and I dream to create fantastic narratives using the latest technology.

I'm currently trained in the art of videography, game design and web development. I'm a self starter with a drive for efficiency and quality. But above all, the story must be worthwhile.

Whether fictitious or historical, I can create and distribute your story in a variety of formats.

Growing up in southern Ontario, I moved around a lot and I've had many meaningful encounters and experiences with people from all walks of live. I flourish when I'm learning something new and love to widen my skill set.

I love a challenge and I plan to push boundaries using new and exciting narrative technologies.


- Zachary Poirier
